- Purpose: To prepare and practice the presentation for the proof of concept demonstration to TAs/Instructor, and to create the accompanying slides.
- Attendees: Full team

- Overview of Proof of Concept: Recap of the core features and functionalities of the Expense Simplification and Management Application to be demonstrated.

- Presentation Structure:
Introduction: Background and objectives of the project.
Problem Statement: Description of the problem the application solves.
Solution Overview: How the application addresses the problem.
Demonstration: Live demo or walkthrough of what we had completed thsu far.
Q&A: Allocating time for questions from TAs/Instructors.

- Practice Session:
Initial run-through of the presentation to assess timing and flow.
Feedback from team members on clarity, content, and delivery.
